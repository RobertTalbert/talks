Flipped learning

The traditional model

Flipped Learning

- First contact with new concepts moves from the group learning space to the individual learning space in the form of structured activity

- The resulting group space is used for active, collaborative work on creative application problems

- Educator's role is to guide and coach students, observe, collect data, and give feedback.
